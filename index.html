About Us

Our passion is providing the highest quality of products to ensure only the best tasting meat and poultry end up on your plate. We offer of a large range of both traditional and American contemporary cuts of lamb, goat and poultry. We provide different types of the high- quality cuts of meats both over the counter for the general public and wholesale to over different local restaurants and delicatessens.

We are one of the top free-range butchers in Milpitas. Our ready-to-cook range is a significant facet of our business. Each one of our home-style recipe meals are made in our kitchen, by hand with gourmet meats daily. All our professional butchers maintain the highest sanitation standards and procedures.

our Process

Our dedicated food safety team scrutinizes every aspect of our operations to make sure we meet local and provincial standards, from how meat is received and stored, to preparation and delivery.
